Features

Built for the way engineering
students actually study

From lab submission to semester promotion — every step is tracked, evaluated, and automated.

Semester Lab Management

Labs are automatically assigned based on your semester and branch. Open your dashboard and they are already there — no manual setup required.

AI Code Evaluation

Submit your code and get a score out of 100 instantly. Google Gemini evaluates correctness, approach, and attempt number — all without manual grading.

Progress Dashboard

Your dashboard shows compulsory labs, practice labs, scores, attendance, and your live semester promotion status — all fetched from the database in real time.

Missed Lab Recovery

Marked absent for a session? Missed labs appear in a separate tab. Submit before the deadline and your attendance updates automatically to Present (Virtual).

Ask AI Learning Mode

Generate MCQs from lab content at any difficulty level. Ask doubts and get answers pulled directly from your lab material — not from the open internet.

Semester Promotion

Pass 60% or more of compulsory labs and your semester status updates automatically. No manual verification — the system handles it.

How It Works

Four steps from signup
to semester cleared

1

Sign Up and Login

Register with your Student ID, semester, and branch. Faculty uses a separate registration path. After login you land directly on your personal dashboard.

2

View Your Assigned Labs

Your dashboard automatically loads all labs for your semester — compulsory, practice, and missed labs — pulled live from the database every time you log in.

3

Submit Code and Get Scored

Open a lab, read the problem statement, type your code, and submit. Gemini evaluates it and returns your score, feedback, and pass or fail status immediately.

4

Get Promoted to Next Semester

Once 60% of compulsory labs are passed, your semester status updates automatically. You see it on your dashboard — Passed and eligible for the next semester.

About

What is SmartVirtualLab?

SmartVirtualLab is a cloud-based virtual lab ecosystem built for engineering colleges. It replaces paper-based lab records and manual evaluation with a fully digital, AI-assisted workflow.

Students submit code directly on the platform. Faculty assigns labs, marks attendance, and reviews scores. Google Gemini evaluates every submission automatically — making the process faster and more consistent.

The system tracks each student's progress in real time and automatically determines semester promotion eligibility based on lab completion percentage.
